Alcohol

For thousands of years, humans have been drinking alcoholic beverages for recreational, medical, and ritual purposes, showing no signs of being phased out from society. In fact, every independent civilization ends up developing and drinking some sort of alcoholic beverage, even without any knowledge of other societies that do so. Ethanol, the main ingredient of alcoholic beverage aside from water, has an active effect on the brain: in small doses, this provides a relaxing effect that increases social function, but after the body is overwhelmed with alcohol, cognitive function is significantly decreased, with loss of balance and slurred speech being common symptoms.
